DiamondCut est 1998

Founded in 1998, DiamondCut Motors has established itself as Indonesia's premier luxury car reseller, providing an exceptional selection of prestigious vehicles from renowned brands such as Porsche, Mercedes-Benz, Bugatti, and many others. Over the years, we have grown exponentially, driven by our passion for delivering the finest automobiles and an unparalleled customer experience. Our showroom features a diverse collection of high-performance and elegant vehicles, catering to the discerning tastes of our clientele. Whether you are looking for the thrill of a sports car, the comfort of a luxury sedan, or the prestige of an exotic vehicle, DiamondCut Motors has something to offer every automotive enthusiast
