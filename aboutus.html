About Us

Brief History

Since its founding in 1998, DiamondCut Motors has emerged as Indonesia's premier destination for luxury vehicles, offering a distinguished selection of renowned brands such as Porsche, Mercedes-Benz, Bugatti, and many others. With a reputation built on excellence and a deep commitment to quality, DiamondCut Motors has been growing exponentially, consistently meeting the high expectations of our discerning clientele. Our extensive showroom showcases a wide range of luxurious vehicles, each selected for its superior performance, elegance, and cutting-edge technology.
